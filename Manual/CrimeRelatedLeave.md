# Crime Related Death and Disappearance Leave

> The Rule of Thumb

*If your child disappears, you don't need to be here - you need to be looking for them.*

This is another unpaid, job protected leave under the ESA. Frogs are entitled up to fifty-two (52) weeks of unpaid job protected leave in the case of the disappearance of their child due to probable crime.
